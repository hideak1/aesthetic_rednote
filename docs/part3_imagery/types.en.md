# 3.4 Cover Image Types — Pick the Right Format and Double Your Click-Through Rate

Different content categories call for different cover formats. Choosing the right type boosts click-through rate far more than just improving image quality.

---

## Five Major Cover Types at a Glance

| Type | Characteristics | Best Categories | Click Power |
|------|----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Person-featured | Clear face, expressive emotion | Outfits, beauty, vlogs | High |
| Product flat lay | Items neatly arranged, shot from above | Hauls, unboxing, favorites | Medium-high |
| Comparison | Side-by-side or top-bottom before/after | Tutorials, reviews, makeovers | High |
| Text poster | Bold text, information-first design | Tips, guides, lists | High |
| Scene & atmosphere | Lifestyle setting, immersive feel | Home decor, travel, food | Medium |

Humans are naturally drawn to faces. Covers featuring a person grab attention more easily in the feed. Keep the face clear, expressions compelling, and the background simple.

!!! tip "When to Use"

!!! warning "Watch Out"
    Avoid over-filtering to the point of looking unreal. Users increasingly value authenticity.

## Comparison: Naturally Attention-Grabbing

<div class="cover-mockup" style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 0;">
  <div style="background: #ddd; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-size: 0.75rem; color: #888; border-right: 1px dashed var(--border-color);">Before</div>
  <div style="background: #FCE4EC; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-size: 0.75rem; color: #880E4F;">After</div>
</div>

People are instinctively curious about change. Showing "before" and "after" in a single image creates powerful visual impact. Make the contrast obvious, keep shooting conditions consistent, and label it clearly.

!!! tip "When to Use"
    Makeup tutorials, skincare reviews, room makeovers, outfit comparisons, organization transformations.

!!! warning "Credibility Is Everything"
    Do not exaggerate results by manipulating lighting or angles. If users suspect dishonesty, the backlash will be severe.

Put the core message directly on the cover so users see the topic and value while still in the feed. Text must be large and legible at thumbnail size, messaging concise, and colors harmonious.

!!! tip "When to Use"
    Tips and tutorials, guide compilations, knowledge sharing, warning lists. Especially effective for "save-worthy" content.

## How to Choose the Right Cover Type for You

Consider three factors: **content category** (match to use cases above), **competition** (see what top-performing posts use), and **personal strengths** (comfortable on camera? use person-featured; strong design skills? lean into text posters).

!!! tip "The Ultimate Advice"
    Do not limit yourself to one cover type — switch flexibly based on content. But within a category, keep styles consistent for a polished profile.

---

## Chapter Summary

- **Person-featured and comparison covers** deliver the highest click-through rates but demand the best source material
- **Text posters** are powerful for tips-and-guides content
- **Product flat lays and atmospheric scenes** rely on aesthetic sense and composition skills
- Weigh your content category, competitor benchmarks, and personal strengths when choosing
