## Subtitle Essentials

- **Keep subtitle font consistent** with your cover font
- **Placement:** Bottom third or top — never block faces or key visuals
- **Color:** White as default, highlight keywords with your brand color
- **Size:** Must be readable on a phone — don't go too small
