# Part 3: Imagery — The Visual Code That Makes People Click

**Great content paired with great images is what truly gets seen. This part helps you master image selection, editing, arrangement, and cover design.**

---

## Overview

On Xiaohongshu, images are the primary currency. Whether a post gets clicked depends 80% on the cover image; whether it gets read through depends on the quality and arrangement of every single photo. Choosing images is not a casual afterthought — it is a complete visual strategy.

This part will systematically help you build an imagery mindset, covering selection criteria, editing and filters, multi-image arrangement, and cover image types.

## What You Will Learn

- What makes an image worth selecting
- How to unify visual style through editing and filters
- The logic and rhythm of arranging multiple images
- Which cover image types work best for different content categories

## Chapters

| # | Chapter | Key Topics |

| 3.4 | [Cover Image Types](types.en.md) | Person-featured, product flat lay, comparison, text poster, scene & atmosphere |

## Suggested Learning Path

Work through the chapters in order: first master the fundamentals of image selection, then learn to unify your style with color grading, understand the rhythm of multi-image arrangement, and finally choose the right cover type for your content category.

After each chapter, practice with your own images — hands-on experimentation is far more effective than reading alone.
