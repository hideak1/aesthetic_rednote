## Rhythm Essentials

- **First 3 seconds are life or death:** Open with the result or spark curiosity
- **Optimal Xiaohongshu video length:** 30 seconds to 1 minute
- **Cut every 2-3 seconds** to maintain pace
- **Use trending platform BGM** for a boost
