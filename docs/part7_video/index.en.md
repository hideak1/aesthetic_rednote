# Part 7: Video Aesthetics

Xiaohongshu videos need aesthetic design just like image posts. The cover determines clicks, the first 3 seconds determine retention, and subtitles affect watch-through rate.

---

## Chapter Overview

### 7.1 Video Covers

Video covers matter even more than image post covers — because videos display a thumbnail by default in the feed. Random frame grabs vs designed covers make a huge difference in click-through rate.

**Keywords:** Cover design · Thumbnails · Style consistency

---

### 7.2 Editing Rhythm

The first 3 seconds are do-or-die. Xiaohongshu video pacing is completely different from long-form video — fast cuts, high density, show the result first.

**Keywords:** 3-second hook · Fast editing · Watch-through rate

---

### 7.3 Text & Subtitles

Subtitles are video's "typography" — just as important as text layout in image posts. Font, color, and placement all matter.

**Keywords:** Subtitle styles · Font consistency · Keyword highlights

---

## How to Use

!!! tip "Video and image posts are one system"
    Don't treat video as a separate thing. Everything you learned about color, composition, and layout in Parts 1-6 applies to video too.

Every chapter leads with **phone mockup demos** — see the result first, learn the method second.
