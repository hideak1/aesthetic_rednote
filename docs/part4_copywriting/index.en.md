# Part 4: Copywriting Aesthetics

A beautiful cover gets the click, but beautiful copy keeps the reader. Copywriting on Xiaohongshu is more than just words — it is a blend of visual rhythm, information design, and search strategy. This part teaches you how to craft titles, body text, and keywords that are both pleasing to read and powerful in reach.

---

## Chapter Overview

### 4.1 The Art of Titles

The title is the very first sentence a user reads — sometimes even more decisive than the cover image. We share 5 high-CTR title formulas that help you write headlines with both substance and magnetism.

### 4.2 Body Text Rhythm

The body is where your core value lives, but if the layout is cluttered and the information density overwhelming, readers will simply scroll away. Learn how paragraph pacing, visual dividers, and strong openings and closings make your text as comfortable to read as breathing.

### 4.3 Elegant Keyword Embedding

Search traffic on Xiaohongshu is too important to ignore. How do you weave keywords into your title and body naturally — without sacrificing readability — so that more people discover your content? This chapter teaches you precision keyword placement.
